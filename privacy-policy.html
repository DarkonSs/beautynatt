Privacy Policy

Welcome to BeautyNatt, a beauty and personal care affiliate marketing website. Our main goal is to provide valuable content to our users and help them find the best products and services for their needs.

To achieve this, we collect certain information from our users, which we use to improve our website and provide the best possible user experience. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information Collection and Use

At BeautyNatt, we collect information from our users through registration and contact forms. We use this information to notify our registered users about new content such as product reviews, product comparisons, beauty and personal care advice and tips, and much more. We also store the information provided by our users to fill the fields of the forms with that data as default for the next time the users want to use the forms.

We use Google Analytics to track the performance and usage of our website. This tool helps us understand important metrics such as the number of visitors, which pages are visited the most, and how much time users spend on our site. This information is shared with Google and may be used in accordance with their privacy policy. However, we would like to assure you that we do not use Google Analytics to collect or store any personally identifiable information.
